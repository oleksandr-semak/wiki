## Overview

The AWS Certified Solutions Architect – Professional (SAP-C02) exam validates advanced technical skills and experience in designing distributed applications and systems on the AWS platform.

## Exam Details

- **Exam Code:** SAP-C02
- **Duration:** 180 minutes
- **Questions:** 75 questions
- **Passing Score:** 750/1000
- **Cost:** $300 USD

## Domains

1. **Design Solutions for Organizational Complexity** (26%)
2. **Design for New Solutions** (29%)
3. **Continuous Improvement for Existing Solutions** (25%)
4. **Accelerate Workload Migration and Modernization** (20%)
